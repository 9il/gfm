D translation of  stb_image-1.33 (http://nothings.org/stb_image.c)

This port only supports:

  • PNG 8-bit-per-channel only.
  • JPEG baseline (no JPEG progressive).
  • GIF.
  • BMP non-1bpp, non-RLE.
